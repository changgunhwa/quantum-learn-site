About This Project

This website introduces ongoing quantum sensing and communication research.

Core Technology

We are developing a new type of microwave-based quantum radar, incorporating quantum entanglement, quantum spin control, and quantum dot technologies. Our system operates without the need for complex quantum receivers or optical-to-microwave conversion systems, enabling energy-efficient and real-time detection under all conditions.

Key Features

  • Direct quantum spin control with enhanced diffraction sensitivity
  • Microwave-based quantum sensing with 100% energy efficiency
  • Consistent detection performance across all distances
  • Real-time response with minimal power consumption

Device Specifications

  • Detection Range: 1 km
  • Detection Angle: 90°
  • Target Speed: ≥ 0.8 m/s
  • Power Consumption: 10 mW
  • Detectable Objects: Vehicles, drones, humans, animals (including birds)
  • Radar Type: Doppler radar
  • Battery: 1000mAh / 7.4V lithium-ion (up to 5 days)
  • Solar Power: Self-charging outdoors, no external power required

Development Roadmap

  • Long-range detection capability: 100 km+
  • Stealth object detection verification
  • Deployment for both military and civilian applications
